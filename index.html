Who We Are

About Duque Workforce Connect

We believe that finding steady farm work should be a seamless experience. Duque Workforce Connect is the premier gateway for harvesting labourers looking to join fruit, vegetable, and specialty crop farms across the country. We specialize in connecting reliable, hard-working individuals with growers and orchards, ensuring that your effort meets the right opportunity.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between reliable harvesting labourers and growers by providing tailored farm placement solutions that foster steady income, fair treatment, and long-term working relationships.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted workforce catalyst in the agriculture sector, transforming the way fruit, vegetable, and specialty crop farms find dependable harvest labour through integrity, dedication, and honest matching.

Perks That Support Every Harvest

We provide more than just job listings; we provide steady, seasonal, and long-term work you can count on. Partnering with Duque Workforce Connect unlocks perks designed to support your income, your safety, and your well-being on the farm.

Steady Placements

Gain direct access to fruit, vegetable, and specialty crop farms hiring across the growing and harvest seasons.

On-the-Job Training

No formal education or prior experience required — get hands-on training in planting, picking, sorting, and packing.

Accommodation Support

Community Support

Join a network of harvesting labourers for mentorship, peer support, and guidance throughout the season.
