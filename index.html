How It Works

Your Application Journey, Step By Step

Starting an application shouldn't feel like a leap of faith. Here's exactly what happens from the moment you submit your details to the day your visa is in hand — so you know what to expect at every stage, with no surprises along the way.

STEP 1

Submit Your Application

Fill out our straightforward online application with your work history, credentials, and supporting documents. It only takes a few minutes, and a real person on our team reviews every submission, never an automated filter.

STEP 2

Pre-Assessment

Our recruitment specialists carefully evaluate your qualifications, experience, and career goals to confirm you're a strong match for current opportunities before moving your file forward.

STEP 3

Assessment With Endorsing Company

Shortlisted candidates are introduced to our partner employer for a joint review. This is where the hiring company assesses your fit firsthand and formally endorses you for the role.

STEP 4

Results

You'll receive a clear, timely update on your outcome. Whether it's an offer, next steps, or constructive feedback, we keep you informed instead of leaving you guessing.

STEP 5

Visa Application Process

Once endorsed, our team walks you through the visa or work permit process, helping you prepare the right documentation, meet deadlines, and stay on track for your move to Canada.

Your Success is Our Priority

Beyond placing you in a role, we are committed to helping you grow. From your first day to your next promotion, our team is here to support your career.
